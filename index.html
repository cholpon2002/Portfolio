Mobile App

FinTrack — Personal Finance App

Challenge

FinTrack's onboarding flow had a 67% drop-off rate. Users were overwhelmed by the number of steps and confused by financial jargon. The business needed to cut that number in half.

Process

I conducted 8 user interviews, analyzed session recordings, and ran a heuristic evaluation. Key issues: too many form fields upfront, unclear value proposition, no progress indicator.

Solution

Redesigned onboarding with progressive disclosure — 3 steps instead of 9, contextual explanations, animated micro-interactions, and a single "quick start" path for busy users.

E-commerce

Lumière — Luxury Fashion Platform

Challenge

A luxury fashion brand's e-commerce site felt generic and didn't match the premium experience customers expected. The checkout had a 78% abandonment rate.

Process

Analyzed luxury buyer psychology, audited 6 competitor sites, ran A/B tests on checkout steps, and built a full design system ensuring consistency across 200+ pages.

Solution

Editorial-inspired layouts, streamlined 2-step checkout, trust signals throughout, and a refined design system reflecting the brand's haute couture identity.

Health Tech

Wellpath — Mental Health App

Challenge

Design a mental wellness app that feels safe and calming for users experiencing anxiety or depression — zero clinical coldness, full WCAG AA accessibility.

Process

Consulted with a clinical psychologist, conducted trauma-informed design workshops, and tested with neurodivergent users to validate accessibility.

Solution

Soft, organic shapes, a muted color palette, gentle Lottie animations, and a check-in flow designed around emotional vocabulary rather than clinical terms.

EdTech

Learnly — Online Learning Platform

Challenge

Learner engagement on Learnly was dropping after the first week. Users signed up, completed one lesson, and never returned.

Process

Analyzed behavioral data, interviewed 15 churned users, and studied gamification patterns in Duolingo, Headspace, and Habitica for inspiration.

Solution

Designed a streak system, achievement badges, peer leaderboards, and adaptive feedback that celebrates small wins to build learning habits.
